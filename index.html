About

Twitter2Imgur is a small Windows/Linux app for quickly grabbing the images posted to a Twitter account and uploading them to an Imgur account. It was designed for screenshots shared from a PS4 (full 1080p versions), but is not specific to that purpose. It is written in Free Pascal/Lazarus.
